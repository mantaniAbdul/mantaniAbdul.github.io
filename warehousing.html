Unity Pharmacy Warehousing

One of the factors contributing to the Wholeness of our medical
products is our excellent and well-structured warehousing
facilities. We have one of the most modern warehousing facilities
supported by well trained personnel who ensure all products are
stored and transported under standardized conditions. Our facilities
have been certified by the Ghana Standard Authority as well as the
Food and Drugs Authority.
